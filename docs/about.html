ABOUT US

We Love Our Job To Eternity!

We are a small search agency with a big vision. The vision is not to become the #1 agency on the planet or close five hundred deals a year, or anything like that. The vision is to think beyond money. The vision is to create an impact on our clients’ lives and offer them the most pleasant experience ever. That’s not easy in a world where 99% of the companies we know are part of the rat race. We quit that to become a frugal search agency, and we are now simply focusing on delivering the highest amount of client satisfaction every time.


We want to attain self-sustainability and become purely customer-centric, so we only charge a nominal cost for our services.

Here are a few of our stand-out features.

We offer a free SEO trial.

We're offering a FREE SEO trial for the first time ever, which will enable business owners to first test our service, get an understanding of how it works and then take up a subscription plan.

We take only 5 clients in a quarter.

We're strict to this principle, and do NOT take more than 5 clients in a quarter. It enables us to fully focus on our existing clients and deliver the best service to them.

We respect our clients' privacy.

Our clients' privacy is our top priority. We never ever compromise on that. We not only keep their data secret, but also create a stress-free environment where they enjoy our cooperation.

We stay up-to-date.

There are a million things happening in the digital-tech industry every single moment. It's not easy to stay informed of all of that, but we keep track of the news that is important and necessary.

We are small and frugal.

We don't own a huge office space or super expensive assets to show off. Our clients don't benefit from that. We rather choose to stay frugal and help our clients enjoy a low-cost deal every time.

We care for our planet and the people.

This year, we've initiated two life-changing campaigns in the name of #SaveEarth and #NoHunger to meet our long-time goals of doing something for the mother earth and the people who suffer from hunger.
